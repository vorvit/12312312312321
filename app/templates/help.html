Welcome to IFC Auth Service!

This service allows you to manage IFC files, view 3D models, and collaborate with your team.

  • Dashboard: Overview of your account and recent activity
  • Files: Upload, manage, and view your IFC files
  • Profile: Manage your account settings

Working with IFC Files

IFC (Industry Foundation Classes) files are used for building information modeling (BIM).

  • Upload: Drag and drop files or use the upload button
  • View: Click the eye icon to open files in the 3D viewer
  • Download: Click the download icon to save files locally
  • Delete: Click the trash icon to remove files

Managing Your Account
  • Profile Settings: Update your personal information
  • Password: Change your password for security
  • Sessions: View and manage active login sessions
  • Storage: Monitor your file storage usage
